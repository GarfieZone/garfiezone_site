Before 20205 ended I was going to write a post looking back on all the things I've done or that have happened in 2025 that I liked or whatever. A way to look back positively. And then I didn't write it. And then 2026 happened, and I woke up feeling really depressed and unaccomplished. And then I felt better. And now I'm going back to the idea of that post. So I guess here it goes, I hope there's actually enough to make me feel okay about the year.

  • I designed sound for a play that my sweetie directed
  • I designed sound for a music at the university I used to go to
  • I designed sound and composed music and directed an ensemble for my first Shakespeare play at the same university
  • I ran sound for a cabaret at the theatre I used to work at, and might continue doing that for their future cabarets

  • Started working through The Odin Project. I stalled out in the CSS section, but I plan on continuing it. Also wanting to work through freeCodeCamp's offerings.

Oh boy, not coming up with much. Maybe I'll add more if I can think of it. But I feel like I'm in the same spot I was a year ago, and not feeling great about it! Will I ever learn how to live a life? Tune in next year to find out!
